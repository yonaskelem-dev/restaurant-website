Our Story

A Journey Through
Ethiopian Heritage

Sishu was born from a simple belief: that food is the most honest expression of a people's soul. Founded in 2012 by Chef Almaz Haile, our kitchen has always been a bridge between the timeless traditions of Ethiopian cuisine and the energy of a modern Addis.

Every injera is fermented for 72 hours. Every berbere blend is ground by hand. We don't cut corners because our grandmothers never did.

🌾

Farm to Table

Sourced daily from local markets and trusted farms across Oromia and Amhara regions.
